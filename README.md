# 📚 Shravya Tutor – Smart Home Tuition & Learning Platform

Welcome to **Shravya Tutor**, an all-in-one modern learning platform designed to connect students with personalized education journeys — from **Nursery & Primary Classes** to **Board Exam Preparation** and **Advanced Professional Courses** like **Python, Data Science, AI & Machine Learning**.

Built with the vision of making quality education accessible, affordable, and engaging for every learner.

---

## 🚀 Vision

To create a powerful digital tutoring ecosystem where students of every age and level can learn from expert tutors, structured courses, and AI-powered personalized study paths.
